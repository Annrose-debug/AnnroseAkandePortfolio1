About Me

Hi, I'm Annrose Akande — a passionate and curious tech enthusiast who enjoys turning ideas into real, working applications. I’m currently exploring software development, web development, and data, and I’m always looking for ways to challenge myself and grow.

I recently completed a Computer Programming program at Algonquin College, where I built a strong foundation in developing responsive, user-focused web applications. I’ve worked on projects ranging from interactive web apps to landing pages and e-commerce interfaces. My core stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, TypeScript, and React, along with experience in Java, Python, and MySQL.

Beyond coding, I enjoy solving problems and pushing through challenges until things finally click. I have a growing interest in cybersecurity and modern AI tools, and I’m always exploring how they can be used to build smarter, more efficient solutions.

Outside of tech, I enjoy watching action and spy movies, exploring new ideas, and finding inspiration for my next project. I’m naturally curious and always thinking about how things work — whether it’s in technology or everyday life.

My goal is to become a full-stack developer and eventually build impactful, globally recognized tech solutions. Right now, I’m focused on learning, building, and becoming the best developer I can be — one project at a time.
